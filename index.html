About Me

I design systems that have a positive impact on people or a system. This involves user research, domain exploration, brainstorming, wireframing, prototyping, evaluating, etc.

Currently I am pursuing my Masters degree at Georgia Tech in Human-Computer Interaction, focusing on ubiquitous computing, data, and interaction design.

In addition to thinking about the future and designing new things, I mountain bike, hike and run with my dog, scuba dive, and explore new places.
