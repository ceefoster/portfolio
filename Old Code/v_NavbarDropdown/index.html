I am interested in a combination of human-computer interaction, ubiquitous/pervasive computing, and data. I want to design and develop systems that have a positive impact on people or an ecosystem.

I am currently a student at Georgia Tech, pursuing my Masters degree in Human-Computer Interaction. I am working as a research assistant with Professor Lauren Klein to study how information visualization and people's understanding and acceptance of data has changed over history.

When I am not studying and working, I enjoy spending time outside, mountain biking, hiking, running, and scuba diving.
