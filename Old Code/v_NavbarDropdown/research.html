Digital Humanities: Historical Information Visualization

Time Period: Fall 2015 - Spring 2016

I am currently working with Professor Lauren Klein in the Digital Humanities Lab to learn from historical visualizations. We are studying how these artifacts and people's understanding of them have changed over time.

Public Lab: Drones and Foraging

Time Period: Summer 2013 - Spring 2014

I worked with Professor Carl DiSalvo and two other students to explore the use of DIY drones and urban foraging. For this speculative design project, I developed use cases, visualized fruit data with D3, and learned about hobby drone technology.
